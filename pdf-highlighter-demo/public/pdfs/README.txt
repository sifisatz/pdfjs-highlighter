This folder contains the PDFs used by the comparison demo:

- receipt.pdf      (left viewer – Instruction)
- statement.pdf    (right viewer – Confirmation)

If you replace the demo files, keep the same filenames:

- Copy your receipt PDF to  `public/pdfs/receipt.pdf`
- Copy your statement PDF to `public/pdfs/statement.pdf`

The app will load them from:
- /pdfs/receipt.pdf
- /pdfs/statement.pdf
